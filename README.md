# The Difference Field and Emergent Invariants

> **A research repository for developing and disseminating materials on the hypothesis that space, time, information, and the subject may emerge from a more fundamental field of distinctions through invariance and compression.**

## About this repository

This repository is dedicated to the development, discussion, and dissemination of articles, notes, formalizations, and related materials connected with the conceptual framework:

**The Difference Field and Emergent Invariants**

At the current stage, this project should be understood as an early conceptual research program rather than a completed theory. Its central proposal is that reality may not fundamentally consist of ready-made objects located in space and time. Instead, there may exist a more primitive **Difference Field** (`D`), from which stable structures, information, space, time, and the subject (`S`) emerge through multilevel compression and the formation of invariants ($\mathcal{I}$).

The purpose of this repository is to make these materials openly available for critical examination, refinement, translation, and interdisciplinary discussion.

---

## Main conceptual orientation

The framework explored here is based on several working ideas:

- **Difference Field (`D`)** — a pre-object field of distinctions that is not yet organized into objects, space, time, or meaning.
- **Invariant (`I`)** — a stable form obtained through the compression of differences.
- **System of invariants ($\mathcal{I}$)** — a structured set of invariants available to a subject.
- **Subject (`S`)** — a higher-order invariant structure that maintains its own continuity while integrating an accessible world.
- **Compression** — the process through which a limited system reduces the complexity of the Difference Field and stabilizes distinctions into invariants.

Within this perspective, the accessible world of a subject is not identical to the fullness of reality, but is instead a structured and limited manifestation of the Difference Field.

## Current status

This project is currently at an early and exploratory stage.

It includes:

* conceptual articles;
* working terminology and notation;
* first attempts at formal structuring;
* interdisciplinary links to cybernetics, information theory, neuroscience, and philosophy of mind.

It does **not yet** include:

* a complete mathematical formalism;
* rigorous proofs;
* experimentally validated predictions;
* a finalized relation to established physical theories.

For this reason, the materials in this repository should be treated as open working documents intended for criticism, refinement, and further development.

---

## Research direction

Although the framework is still preliminary, it is intended to stimulate new hypotheses and possible lines of inquiry in areas such as:

* fundamental physics;
* mathematics;
* cybernetics;
* neuroscience and biology in general;
* gerontology;
* the study and search for intelligence or subjectivity, including non-human and non-terrestrial cases;
* artificial intelligence.

## Notes

This repository is intended as a place for publishing and developing research materials, not as a claim that the framework presented here is already complete or verified. Its primary purpose is to introduce a conceptual perspective that may later support more rigorous formal, physical, mathematical, and interdisciplinary work.
